## 1. AI-Assisted Diagnostician

**Pattern Focus:** Analytical + Patience + Helping

**The Character:** Dr. Amara Osei, 34, trained as radiologist, now specializes in AI-human diagnostic collaboration

### Crossroads Scene
```
Amara: "The AI flagged a shadow on the scan. 99.2% confidence: benign cyst. 
But something feels off to me. The shape. I've seen thousands of these.

If I order more tests, I delay treatment, rack up costs, maybe scare the patient 
for nothing. If I trust the AI, we move forward.

The AI has been right more often than me. Statistically. But this time..."
```

**Choices:**
- "Trust your gut. Order the tests." (Patience + Analytical)
- "The AI's track record speaks for itself." (Analytical)
- "Talk to the patient. Let them decide." (Helping)
- "What specifically feels off?" (Exploring)

### Day-in-Life Pressure Test
```
SCENE: Diagnostic Review Room, 7 cases queued

Timer: 45 minutes until tumor board meeting

Case 1: AI says pneumonia. Textbook presentation. [CONFIRM / REVIEW]
Case 2: AI uncertain (67% liver mass, 33% artifact). [ESCALATE / DISMISS]
Case 3: AI missed something you caught. Do you report the error? [REPORT / NOTE PRIVATELY]
Case 4: Patient's 5th scan this year. AI recommends 6th. [APPROVE / PUSH BACK]

Each choice reveals: How do you balance efficiency vs. thoroughness?
```

### Ethical Dilemma
```
"The hospital wants to reduce our review time by 40%. 
'AI handles the obvious cases, you handle exceptions.'

But the exceptions are where I learn. Where the AI learns.
If I'm not seeing normal, how do I recognize abnormal?"
```

### Unexpected Moment
```
A patient's daughter catches Amara in the hallway.

"My mom says you're the one who found it early. The AI missed it."

Amara: "The AI... helped narrow things down."

"She says you looked at the scan for twenty minutes. That you saw her, not just the image."
```

How hard do you push? How creative do you get?

# IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

## Character Selection for Station 2
Recommend 8 characters covering:
- 2 Healthcare (AI Diagnostician, Crisis Responder)
- 2 Tech (AI Safety, Robotics Integration)
- 2 Green (Circular Economy, Sustainable Ag)
- 2 Creative (Narrative Designer, AI Artist)

## Crossroads Scenes
- Each 3-5 nodes
- Always 4 choices (one per relevant pattern)
- No "right" answer—values revealed through choice

## Day-in-Life Pressure Tests
- Timed decisions (real-time or compressed)
- Multiple simultaneous demands
- Trade-offs made visible after choice
- Pattern revealed by WHAT you prioritize

## Ethical Dilemmas
- No clear answer
- Professional stakes
- Personal stakes
- Player's pattern affects how question is framed

## Unexpected Moments
- Humanize the professional
- Connect work to life
- Show why this career matters beyond money
- Often involve relationship (patient, student, colleague)
