## General Principles

### Dialogue Patterns for Emotion

**Hesitation:**
- Ellipses: "I... I don't know."
- Fragments: "Pre-med. Second year."

**Anxiety:**
- Fragments: "Biochemistry notes. Robotics parts. Everywhere."
- Repetition: "It's going great. Really great."

**Weight/Importance:**
- Repetition: "Good. That's what this place is for. Good."
- Short sentences: "I've been here a long time. Longer than I expected."

**Warmth:**
- Complete sentences: "Welcome. I'm Samuel Washington."
- Measured pace: Longer, thoughtful sentences

### What to Remove

**Stage Directions:**
- `*text*` (italic)
- `_text_` (underscore italic)
- "A pause"
- "His voice breaks"
- "Studies you"
- "Not judgment. Understanding."

**Actions:**
- "This isn't just a job."
- "Something settles in you"
- "carries weight"
- Any description of how character acts/reacts

**Abstract Language:**
- "discover about yourself"
- "find your path"
- "what you discover"
- "meaningful change"
