# Faction Spec: The Market Brokerage
**Role**: The Neutral Arbiters
**Core Philosophy**: "Everything has a price. Even the truth."

---

## 1. Sensory Pillars

### A. Visual Identity
*   **Palette**: Gold, Velvet Purple, Deep Shadows.
*   **Shape Language**: Arches, curtains, hidden pockets, intricate keys.
*   **Architecture**: "Pop-up" luxury. Tents made of expensive drone silk inside ruined corridors.
*   **UI Style**: Ledger books, gold serif fonts, wax seals.

### B. Audio Identity
*   **Instrumentation**: Piano, shuffling cards, clinking coins.
*   **Ambience**: Hushed whispers, low jazz, the sound of tea being poured.
*   **Voice Processing**: Smooth, persuasive, confident.

### C. Linguistic Identity
*   **Naming Convention**: Transactional roles or ironic titles. (e.g., "The Broker", "The Auditor", "Penny").
*   **Keywords**: Value, Investment, Risk, Leverage, Debt, Asset.
*   **Taboo**: "Free", "Charity", "Loyalty" (without payment).

---

## 2. Cultural Structure

### Economy
*   **Currency**: "Secrets" (Knowledge Flags).
*   **Value**: Dangerous information is high value. Gossip is low value.
*   **Trade**: Barter system. Information for Access/Goods.

### Hierarchy
*   **Leader**: The Broker (Face unseen).
*   **Classes**:
    *   *Appraisers*: Valuate the secrets.
    *   *Collectors*: Acquire the secrets (Spies).
    *   *Vault-Keepers*: Guard the data.

---

## 3. History & Beliefs

### The Myth
"Before the Brokerage, people killed for secrets. Now they trade for them. We brought Peace through Profit."

### The Hypocrisy (Internal Contradiction)
They claim to be neutral, but they manipulate the market to keep the war between Technocrats and Naturalists going forever (because war is profitable).

---

## 4. Current Agenda
*   **Goal**: Acquire the "Prime Code" from Sector 3 (Deep Station).
*   **Obstacle**: Marcus (The System) blocks them.
*   **Conflict**: Hiring the Player to spy on everyone.
