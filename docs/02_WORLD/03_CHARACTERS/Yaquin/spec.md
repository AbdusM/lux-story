# Character Spec: Yaquin
**Role**: The Elder / The Oracle
**Faction**: Outlier (The Void)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: An old man who stares into the vacuum of space and sees patterns.
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: To decipher the "Star Song".
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: He was an astrophysicist before the collapse. Now he uses astrology because people understand it better.
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "The stars care about us."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Sitting cross-legged, floating (zero-g section?). Robes made of foil.
    *   *Colors*: Silver, Void Black, Nebula Purple.
    *   *Key Item**: A telescope made of pipes.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Cryptic, poetic, non-linear.
    *   *Keywords*: Alignment, Void, Signal, Drift, Orbit.
    *   *Taboo*: "Clock Time", "Schedule".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Apophenia. Seeing connections where there are none.
*   **Their Truth**: He actually *did* hear a signal from Earth. Once.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Chart" (Sim: Constellation Mapping). Finding the path home.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: A Prophecy (Hint about the ending).
    *   *Trust Lost*: "Your star is in retrograde." (Bad luck).

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: The Comet (Passing through).
*   *Vs. Marcus*: Yaquin thinks Marcus is a "Machine God". Marcus things Yaquin is "Bugged Code".
*   *Vs. Asha*: They vibe. Art and Magic mix well.
