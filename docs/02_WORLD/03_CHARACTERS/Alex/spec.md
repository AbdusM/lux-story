# Character Spec: Alex
**Role**: The Rat / The Guide
**Faction**: Outlier (Under-City)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: A logistics savant who tracks every resource flow in the station. He knows the "Veins" because he lives in them.
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: A warm bed. A full meal.
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: Abandonment. Parents "went to catch a train" and never came back.
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "I don't need anyone."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Small, agile, hidden in a hood. Too many pockets.
    *   *Colors*: Sewer Greys, Camouflage, Mud.
    *   *Key Item**: A pair of night-vision goggles that are way too big.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Skittish, slang-heavy. "Skedaddle", "Shiny", "Ghost".
    *   *Keywords*: Tunnel, Vent, Shiny, Hide, Run.
    *   *Taboo*: "Home", "Family", "Staying Put".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Survival. Everything is edible or tradeable.
*   **Their Truth**: He found a room where the walls are made of gold. He won't tell anyone.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Shortcut" (Sim: Logistics/Maze). Teaching you how to optimize travel routes and manage stamina resources.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: He shows you the "Secret Path".
    *   *Trust Lost*: He picks your pocket and vanishes.

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: The Mark (initially), then The Big Sibling.
*   *Vs. Samuel*: Alex steals from Samuel, but Samuel pretends not to notice.
*   *Vs. Kai*: Kai tries to seal the vents Alex lives in. War.
