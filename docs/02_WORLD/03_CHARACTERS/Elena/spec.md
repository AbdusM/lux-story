# Character Spec: Elena
**Role**: The Truth / The Librarian
**Faction**: Independent (Deep Station Historian)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: The keeper of the "Old Internet" (or what's left of it).
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: To preserve Context.
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: She saw the "Great Deletion" happen in real-time. The burning of the digital Alexandria.
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "If we remember everything, we won't repeat it." (But memory is paralyzing).

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Surrounded by stacks of physical hard drives and paper books. Cardigan.
    *   *Colors*: Sepia, Dust Grey, Faded Gold.
    *   *Key Item**: A microfiche reader.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Academic, referencing sources. "According to file 7B..."
    *   *Keywords*: Source, Citation, Era, Fragment, Lost.
    *   *Taboo*: "Edit", "Delete", "Rewrite".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Nostalgia. She thinks the past was better just because it was recorded.
*   **Their Truth**: She has the *original* source code for the Station. Infinite power, but she's too afraid to run it.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Research" (Sim: NotebookLM / Perplexity). Synthesizing fractured logs to find a password.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: She lets you check out a "Book".
    *   *Trust Lost*: "Access Restricted. Clearance Level Red."

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: The Student.
*   *Vs. Samuel*: They share tea and look at old photos.
*   *Vs. Technocrats*: They want to digitize (and compress) her archives. She refuses.
