# Character Spec: Asha
**Role**: The Vision / The Artist
**Faction**: Naturalist (The Spirit)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: An artist who paints the world that *could* be, rather than the one that is.
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: To bring color back to the grey Station.
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: She has synesthesia. The "Logic Cascade" (Era 4) was physically painful for herâ€”a wall of screaming static.
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "Beauty can save us."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Flowing fabrics, paint smudges on face, constantly framing shots with hands.
    *   *Colors*: Vibrant Teal, Magenta, Paints derived from crushed berries.
    *   *Key Item**: A digital brush stylus that works on any surface.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Abstract, drifting. She describes smells as colors.
    *   *Keywords*: Hue, Vibration, Flow, Resonate, Dream.
    *   *Taboo*: "Grid", "Line" (straight), "Grey".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Surrealism. She ignores literal danger because it "looks beautiful". (e.g., A toxic gas leak is "a lovely violet mist").
*   **Their Truth**: She sees the "Emotional Heatmap" of the station. She knows where people are hurting.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Canvas" (Sim: Stable Diffusion / Visual Canvas). Designing the mural for the New Entry.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: She paints *you* into the mural.
    *   *Trust Lost*: The world becomes greyscale in her dialogue.

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: The Muse.
*   *Vs. Technocrats*: She terrifies them because she doesn't follow lines.
*   *Vs. Jordan*: A little too loud, but necessary.
