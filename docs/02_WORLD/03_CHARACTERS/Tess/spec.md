# Character Spec: Tess
**Role**: The Merchant / The Survivor
**Faction**: Market (Fringe)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: A market analyst who can spot a bubble before it bursts. She upgrades "Barter" to "Algorithmic Trading".
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: To own her own shop (permanently).
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: She lost everything in the "Market Crash" (Literal structural collapse).
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "Things are safer than people."

---

*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Arms crossed, leaning on a crate. Tough.
    *   *Colors*: Leather Brown, Brass, Deep Green.
    *   *Key Item**: A abacus made of rare gems.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Transactional, quick. "Buy or walk."
    *   *Keywords*: Deal, Trade, Quality, Scam, Price.
    *   *Taboo*: "Gift", "Favor", "Later".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Cynicism. Everyone wants something.
*   **Their Truth**: She has a stash of legitimate "Earth Seeds". Worth billions.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Deal" (Sim: Negotiation). Bartering for a generator part.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
