# Character Spec: Grace
**Role**: The Caretaker / The Mender
**Faction**: Neutral (Medical Bay)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: A doctor trying to cure malnutrition with placebo and kindness.
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: Supplies. Real medicine.
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: She has to choose who gets the last antibiotic every week. The "Triage Guilt".
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "I can save everyone if I just stretch the resources enough."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Lab coat stained with rust (not blood, hopefully). Tired eyes.
    *   *Colors*: Pale Mint Green, White (yellowed), Lavender.
    *   *Key Item**: A digital stethoscope that glitches.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Soft, soothing, but firm. Bedside manner.
    *   *Keywords*: Breathe, Pulse, Stable, Rest, Hydrate.
    *   *Taboo*: "Death", "Terminal", "Hopeless".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Utility. She judges people by their "Health Status". A sick leader is a liability.
*   **Their Truth**: She knows the "Vitamin Packs" are 90% sawdust. She makes them anyway.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Triage" (Sim: Medical Logic). Deciding who gets the meds.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: She gives you a Stim Pack (Energy).
    *   *Trust Lost*: You wait in the waiting room. Forever.

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: The Patient.
*   *Vs. Naturalists*: She needs their herbs.
*   *Vs. Technocrats*: She needs their power. She plays both sides.
