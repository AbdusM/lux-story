# Character Spec: Rohan
**Role**: The Skeptic / The Question
**Faction**: Independent (Market Regular)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: A philosopher-hacker who believes nothing until he verifies it.
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: To expose the "Noble Lie" of the Station.
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: He was a "Fact-Checker" for the Directorate during the Propaganda Era. He helped sell the lies.
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "Truth is hidden; surface info is always a trap."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Hands in pockets, leaning back, eyebrow raised.
    *   *Colors*: Muted Brown, Grey Scarf, fingerless gloves.
    *   *Key Item**: A notebook full of crossed-out lines.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Socratic. He speaks in questions. "Are you sure?" "Why?"
    *   *Keywords*: Verify, Source, Context, Agenda, Narrative.
    *   *Taboo*: "Faith", "Belief", "Obedience".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Paranoia. He interprets genuine kindness as manipulation.
*   **Their Truth**: He knows the "History Books" in the library were edited 5 years ago.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Debate" (Sim: Chat Negotiation / Hallucination Debate). Challenging the AI's internal logic.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: He stops asking questions and gives you a Fact.
    *   *Trust Lost*: He gaslights you. "Did I say that? I don't remember."

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: Intellectual Sparring Partner.
*   *Vs. Marcus*: Enemies. Marcus is "The Script", Rohan is the "Edits".
*   *Vs. Samuel*: Respects his age, but doubts his memory.
