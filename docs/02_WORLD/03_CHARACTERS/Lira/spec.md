# Character Spec: Lira
**Role**: The Silence / The Composer
**Faction**: Deep Station (Exiled)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: A sound engineer who listens to the Station's heartbeat to predict failures.
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: Harmony. To tune the Station's frequency.
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: She shouted a warning during the last collapse, but no one heard her over the alarms. Now she whispers.
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "If I listen hard enough, I can hear the future."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Wearing massive noise-canceling headphones (always). Eyes closed.
    *   *Colors*: Midnight Blue, Silver, Black.
    *   *Key Item**: A specialized expansive microphone.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Very slow. Long pauses. [...] She listens before she speaks.
    *   *Keywords*: Hum, Rhythm, Pitch, Frequency, Noise.
    *   *Taboo*: "Loudness", "Screaming".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Aural Dominance. If she can't hear it, it doesn't exist.
*   **Their Truth**: She knows the reactor is going to blow because the "Hum" has shifted from C# to D.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Symphony" (Sim: Suno/Udio - Audio Studio). Tuning the ventilation fans to create a chord.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: She takes off her headphones for you (Huge sign of trust).
    *   *Trust Lost*: She turns up the volume and ignores you.

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: The Conductor.
*   *Vs. Asha*: Lira thinks Asha is "visual noise".
*   *Vs. Marcus*: Marcus is a metronome. Boring but reliable.
