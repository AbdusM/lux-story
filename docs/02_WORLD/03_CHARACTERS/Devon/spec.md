# Character Spec: Devon
**Role**: The Fix / The Operator
**Faction**: Technocrat (Low-Level / Grunt)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: Guy who just wants to get the job done and go home.
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: A smooth shift. No tickets.
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: Nothing grand. Just years of being yelled at for things he didn't break.
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "It's good enough for government work."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Slouching, tool belt, coffee stain on chest.
    *   *Colors*: Faded Grey, duct tape everywhere.
    *   *Key Item**: A multi-tool that is definitely not regulation.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Casual, dismissive, practical. "Yeah, yeah, hold on."
    *   *Keywords*: Duct tape, Bypass, Rig, Output, Done.
    *   *Taboo*: "Theory", "Philosophy", "Why".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Short-termsm. He fixes the symptom, not the cause.
*   **Their Truth**: He knows how to bypass the "Safety Locks" that Kai obsesses over.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Hotfix" (Sim: Midjourney / Conversational Optimizer). Generating a fake permit to get parts.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: He does "off the books" repairs for you.
    *   *Trust Lost*: "File a ticket, buddy."

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: The Client.
*   *Vs. Maya*: Thinks she overcomplicates things. "Just hit it with a wrench."
*   *Vs. Kai*: Kai gives him headaches with regulations.
