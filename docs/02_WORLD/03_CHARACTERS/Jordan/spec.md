# Character Spec: Jordan
**Role**: The Rally / The Voice
**Faction**: Naturalist (Activist Wing)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: A community organizer who can turn a crowd into a movement.
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: Collective Action. No one left behind.
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: She was trapped in a sealed sector. The only reason she survived was because others engaged the manual override together.
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "If we speak loud enough, they have to listen."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Arms wide, standing on a crate, forward-leaning.
    *   *Colors*: Red armbands, patchwork denim, microphone headset.
    *   *Key Item**: A megaphone (or a hacked PA system).
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Urgent, rhythmic, repetitive for emphasis. "We move NOW. We move TOGETHER."
    *   *Keywords*: Us, We, Action, Now, Change, Together.
    *   *Taboo*: "I", "Me", "Wait", "Later".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Optimism Bias. She underestimates the structural impossibility of her goals.
*   **Their Truth**: She knows the "Silent Majority" is ready to revolt; they just need a signal.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Crisis" (Sim: Chat Negotiation / Crisis Comms). Managing a panic in the Grand Hall.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: You become the "Symbol" of the movement.
    *   *Trust Lost*: She labels you a "Collaborator". The crowd turns.

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: The Champion (or the Traitor).
*   *Vs. Kai*: Jordan creates the crowds Kai fears.
*   *Vs. Asha*: Allies. Asha dreams it, Jordan sells it.
