# Character Spec: Samuel
**Role**: The Conductor / The Limbic Store
**Faction**: Sector 0 (Neutral / Old Guard)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: The Station's emotional memory system personified as a janitor. He helps users navigate the "Limbic Feedback Loop".
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: To guide the "Passengers" (Players) to their correct destination (Emotional Growth).
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: He was the Conductor who didn't let the last train leave during the "Era 3 Panic" (Guilt).
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "If I keep the floor clean, the trains will come back."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Slumped shoulders, leaning on a broom/counter. Soft, rounded.
    *   *Colors*: Faded Navy Blue (Old Uniform), Brass buttons (tarnished).
    *   *Key Item**: An antique Pocket Watch that is always stopped at 11:59.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Slow, deliberate, warm. Uses "Folks" and "Traveler".
    *   *Keywords*: Time, Wait, Patience, Coffee, Fog.
    *   *Taboo*: "The Crash", "The End", "Leaving" (he avoids talking about his own departure).

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: He romanticizes the past. He remembers the "Good Old Days" which were actually authoritarian. He filters out the decay.
*   **Their Truth**: He knows the *exact* layout of the tunnels better than the Technocrats. He knows where the "Backdoors" are.
