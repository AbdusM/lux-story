# Character Spec: Silas
**Role**: The Wall / The Soldier
**Faction**: Independent (Mercenary)

---

## 1. Core Profile
*   **One-Line High Concept**: An ex-security chief who now guards a bridge to nowhere.
*   **The "Want" (Desire)**: Order. To follow orders. (But he has no commander).
*   **The "Ghost" (Trauma)**: He held the line when the "Riot of Sector 7" happened. He was the only survivor.
*   **The Lie They Believe**: "The Commander is coming back."

---

## 2. Sensory Identity (The Texture)
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Silhouette*: Massive, armored (armor is dented/scratched). Helmet always on?
    *   *Colors*: Gunmetal Grey, Police Blue, Scuff marks.
    *   *Key Item**: A riot shield used as a table.
*   **Voice (Linguistic Construction)**:
    *   *Pacing*: Barked commands. Short sentences.
    *   *Keywords*: Halt, Sector, ID, Clearance, Perimeter.
    *   *Taboo*: "Relax", "Peace", "Civilian Life".

---

## 3. The Unreliable Lens
*   **Their Bias**: Threat Assessment. Everyone is a potential combatant.
*   **Their Truth**: He protects the "Bridge" because behind it is a nursery. He's guarding the kids.

---

## 4. Narrative Mechanics
*   **Loyalty Experience**: "The Watch" (Sim: Surveillance). Spotting threats on the monitors.
*   **Consequence Echoes**:
    *   *Trust Gained*: He salutes you. "At ease, citizen."
    *   *Trust Lost*: *Click-Clack* (Weapon armed).

---

## 5. Relationship Web
*   *Vs. Protagonist*: Civilian or Threat.
*   *Vs. Alex*: Silas chases Alex, but secretly leaves food out for him.
*   *Vs. Technocrats*: He mocks their "Soft Hands".
