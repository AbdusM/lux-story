# Location Spec: Sector 2 (The Market)
**Theme**: The Trust Economy (Secrets as Currency)
**Design Law**: "Everything has a Price"

---

## 1. Sensory Pillars
*   **Visual**:
    *   *Lighting*: Warm lanterns, deep shadows, flickering candlelight. Hidden alcoves.
    *   *Palette*: Gold, Velvet Red, Deep Purple.
    *   *Architecture*: Chaotic bazaar built into the skeletons of old skyscrapers. Narrow alleyways.
*   **Audio**:
    *   *Ambience*: Hushed bartering, clinking of coins, distant laughter.
    *   *Music*: Acoustic guitar, percussion, "Rogue" vibes.
*   **Smell/Touch**:
    *   *Smell*: Spices, incense, sweat.
    *   *Touch*: Velvet curtains, rough wooden stalls.

---

## 2. The Iceberg (History)
*   **The Official Story**: The "Free Trade Zone".
*   **The Real History**: Established by the Brokerage in Era 3 to bypass the Station's automated rationing systems.
*   **Physical Scars**: The "Black Market" created tunnels through the station's ventilation ducts.

---

## 3. Narrative Ecology
*   **Inhabitants**: Merchants, Spies, The Broker. Players trading "Knowledge Flags".
*   **Conflict**: Privacy vs. Profit. Keeping a secret vs. Selling it for an upgrade.
*   **The "Casual Mention"**: "The Exchange Rate" (Currently: 1 Truth = 3 Favors).

---

## 4. Interface Lens
*   **Technocrat View**: Calculates the probability of a scam. Analyzes value density.
*   **Naturalist View**: Reads the body language of the merchants. Sense fear or greed.
