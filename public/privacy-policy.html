Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2025

Introduction

Grand Central Terminus ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our career exploration game.

Information We Collect

Game Progress Data

We store the following information locally on your device to provide game functionality:

  • Current scene progress
  • Choice patterns and preferences
  • Career exploration insights
  • Game performance metrics

No Personal Information

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide game functionality and save your progress
  • Adapt the experience to your choices and preferences
  • Generate career insights and recommendations
  • Improve the game experience

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. We do not have access to your data, and it is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The game operates entirely offline and does not share data with external parties.

Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored on your device
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete all game data by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Portability: Export your game data (available through browser developer tools)
  • Objection: Stop using the game at any time

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Client-side encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security updates

Children's Privacy

Our game is designed for Birmingham youth (ages 11-22). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use our game without parental consent.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
