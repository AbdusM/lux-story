## Compression Techniques Applied

### 1. Cut Adverbs & Adjectives
- ~~"thick, messy, and covered in coffee stains"~~ → "Thick. Coffee-stained."
- ~~"motivated, self-directed"~~ → "motivated"

### 2. Sentence Fragments
- "I have 127 students" → "127 students."
- "I'm looking at the data" → "Data:"

### 3. Remove Filler Words
- ~~"And the worst part?"~~ → "Worst part?"
- ~~"But more importantly?"~~ → "More important?"

### 4. Staccato Rhythm with |
- "Revenue up. Completion up. Refunds down."
- "Not just teacher. Educator-entrepreneur."

### 5. Trust Visual Systems
- Emotion: `analytical` instead of describing analytical thinking
- Interaction: `nod` instead of "He nods thoughtfully"

### 6. Preserve Data Precision
- Statistics kept exact: "85% → 23%", "$15K/office"
- Technical terms maintained: "cohort-based", "self-paced"
